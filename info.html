Privacy Policy

The search engine does not store, analyze, or share your queries.

Your queries are encrypted and processed locally in your browser, ensuring they cannot be intercepted or hacked.

All searches are redirected directly to the selected search engine (Google, Yahoo, or Bing) through a simple forwarding mechanism.

• No third-party libraries or trackers.

• The site does not use cookies. We use local storage to save your selected search engine preference for a better user experience.

Thank you for your trust.
