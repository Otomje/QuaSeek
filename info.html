About Us

QuaSeek is a Ukrainian search site powered by Google, designed for fast and simple searching.

It’s constantly evolving — new features are added regularly to enhance usability.

Developed and maintained by a solo creator passionate about building QuaSeek.

Privacy Policy

The search engine does not store, analyze, or share your queries.

Your query isn’t encrypted, but it can’t be intercepted or hacked — everything happens locally in your browser.

All searches are redirected directly to Google through a simple forwarding mechanism.

• No third-party libraries or trackers.

• The site does not use cookies or local data storage.

Thank you for your trust.
