About Us

QuaSeek is a Ukrainian search engine in English, built for speed and simplicity.

Our goal is to make search convenient and accessible for everyone.

Frequently updated, developed by a one-person team.

Privacy Policy

The search engine does not store, analyze, or share your queries.

All searches are redirected directly to Google through a simple forwarding mechanism.

• No third-party libraries or trackers.

• All actions are processed locally in your browser.

• The site does not use cookies or local data storage.

Thank you for your trust.
